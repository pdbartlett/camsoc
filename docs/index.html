Berkshire and Oxfordshire Cambridge Society

About the Society

The Berks and Oxon Cambridge Society is long-established, and offers an active programme of excursions, visits, walks etc.

Fixtures in our calendar include:

  • Annual autumn lunch at Henley Business School (Greenlands) with an external speaker
  • Brief AGM with a pot luck lunch and presentation
  • Freshers' event at a bar in Reading
  • A weekend away in or around May

Partners and guests are always welcome at our varied events.
