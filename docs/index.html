About the Society

The Berks and Oxon Cambridge Society is long-established, and offers an active programme of excursions, visits, walks etc.

Although the events are varied, there are certain ones that are more or less fixtures in our calendar:

  • an annual get together in the autumn, which is typically a lunch at the Henley Business School at Greenlands with an external speaker
  • a brief AGM with a pot luck lunch, which is then followed by a talk or presentation
  • a Freshers' event at a bar in Reading
  • a weekend away in or around May

Events and outings take place both at weekends and on weekdays, and partners and guests are always welcome.
